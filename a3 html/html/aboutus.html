About MealRipple

We are committed to building a world with less hunger and more hope, one meal at a time.

Vision

A world where no one goes to bed hungry, where food waste is minimized, and communities come together
to create sustainable solutions for food security.
We envision a future where every meal shared
creates ripples of positive change.

Mission

To combat hunger by reducing food waste, raising awareness, and empowering communities
through education and action.
We connect surplus food with those in need while promoting sustainable practices that benefit
both people and the planet.

Supporting UN SDG 2: Zero Hunger

To combat hunger by reducing food waste, raising awareness, and empowering communities through education and action. We connect surplus food with those in need while promoting sustainable practices that benefit both people and the planet.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every action we take and every community we serve.

Compassion

We lead with empathy and care for those facing food insecurity, ensuring dignity in every interaction.

Sustainability

We promote practices that protect our planet while addressing hunger and reducing food waste.

Transparency

We operate with openness and accountability, building trust through honest communication.

Community

We believe in the power of collective action and foster connections that create lasting change.
